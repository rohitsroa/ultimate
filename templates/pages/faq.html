Frequently Asked Questions




This section contains a wealth of information, related to common queries. Before submitting your query do check here, if you cannot find an answer to your question, then submit a query.

MEDICAL

What are the timings of the availability of doctors during the office hours?

10:00 am to 5:30 pm (Lunch Break 1:30 pm -2:00 pm)

Which hospitals, labs and pharmacies are on Air India’s panel?

To check the hospitals in our panel kindly visit www.click.airindia.in

How do I get my medical card?

You need to submit the Medical Detail form for self and family members along with passport size photograph.

What is the procedure of getting treatment at a panel hospital?

You need to report to the Air India’s doctor and get a recommendation for the required treatment at the particular panel hospital.

What is the limit in terms of rupees for treatment of my family members?

The limit depends on your base salary and designation. Kindly refer to www.click.airindia.in
FINANCE

At which time of the month is usually the salary transferred to the employee bank account?

The salary will be transferred to the employee bank account at the end of the month.

When will fill Form 16 be available?

Form 16 will be available after 31st of March every year.

How can I apply for a loan?

Fill the form and give a quotation for the loan, the department will sanction the loan according to your entitlement.

From when will my incremented salary be transferred to my bank account?

The salary will be automatically incremented and transferred to your bank account. In case, salary is not incremented then contact Finance department.

If I go for a trip, what would be Daily Allowance and Trip Allowance?

Your daily allowance and trip allowance and class entitlement depends solely on your designation and base salary.
IT

What is the procedure to get login credentials for the online portal?

Fill the form for login credentials and submit it to the IT department.

How to reset the password for the e-mail address?

On the website, enter your email-id click forget password and the new password will be sent to your registered mobile number.

Where do I get the necessary software required for Air India system?

Softwares are available with our FMS team. You may contact the FMS team for the same.

How do I get internet access for my laptop?

Guest login can be created for limited access. For further information, you may contact FMS engineers.

How to create new email-id?

Fill up the form along with your contact details and desired e-mail if and submit it to the IT department.
